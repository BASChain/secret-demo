Demo introduction

  • blockchain technology

    Focusing on blockchain technology, the company has developed a traffic based transmission protocol, based on which traffic can be accurately calculated and charged.

  • decentralized

    The core advantage of the company is to reduce the cost and consumption at the geometric level through decentralized technology.

  • distributed data transmission

    Products can be used in distributed data transmission, power transmission and other fields.

  • point-to-point

    This technology ensures that no centralized billing server is needed, so that users with close physical distance and far logical distance can realize accurate billing through point-to-point technology, which greatly reduces the cost of equipment use and equipment loss.

  • present business

    At present, network infrastructure service providers set up equipment, maintain equipment, and get profits from users, and to some extent, they can monopolize the industry. But at the same time, the investment of hardware and infrastructure also reduces their income.

  • centralized billing mode

    Under the traditional centralized billing mode, it causes huge bandwidth or power waste. In many cases, users who are physically close to each other need to transmit the traffic and power to the central server and then back to each other in order to accurately measure the user's consumption and billing.

  • new protocol

    Under the new protocol, operators only need to wholesale traffic and develop standards for hardware equipment or sell hardware equipment directly, and users manage equipment and operation traffic. Like Uber drivers, follow the same rules and work for themselves.

  • reduced costs

    And because the central billing service is omitted, point-to-point data or power transmission is realized, the loss is greatly reduced, and the cost of geometric progression is reduced.
